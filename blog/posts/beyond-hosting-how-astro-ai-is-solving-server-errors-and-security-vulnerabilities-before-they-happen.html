What Exactly is Astro AI?

1. Autonomous Error Detection & "Self-Healing" 🛠️

Most hosting platforms wait for something to break before they fix it. Astro AI monitors server heartbeats in real-time.

Cyber threats are evolving, and traditional firewalls aren't enough. Astro AI uses behavioral analysis to protect your site:
