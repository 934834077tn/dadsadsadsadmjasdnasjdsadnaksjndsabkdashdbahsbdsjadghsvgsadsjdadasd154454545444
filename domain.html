Frequently asked questions

What is a domain name?

A domain name is your website's address on the internet (like nutro.cloud). It's what people type in their browser to find you.

How long does registration take?

Domain registration is instant. Once payment is confirmed, your domain is active within 1-2 minutes.

Can I transfer my domain here?

Yes! We support domain transfers from any registrar. The process takes 5-7 days and includes a free 1-year extension.

What's included with every domain?

Every domain includes free WHOIS privacy protection, SSL certificate, DNS management, and email forwarding at no extra cost.

What happens if a domain shows as unavailable?

Our check is a best-effort lookup. If you believe a domain should be available, you can still proceed to checkout where we do a final real-time verification with the registry.
