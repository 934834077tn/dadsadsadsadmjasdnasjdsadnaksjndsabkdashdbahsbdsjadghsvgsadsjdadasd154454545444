Payment & Billing Policy

1. Monthly Payments

All VPS services are billed on a monthly basis. Customers are required to pay the standard monthly price according to the selected plan. Payments must be made on or before the renewal date to ensure uninterrupted service.

Example pricing:

Plan Monthly Price
VPS G1 $6.64
VPS G 3 $15.00

2. Activation & Reactivation Fees

An activation fee applies when creating a new VPS. If a service is suspended due to overdue payments, a reactivation fee may be applied to resume service.

  • Activation fees are a one-time payment per new service.
  • Reactivation fees are only charged when resuming a suspended service.
  • Paying before the renewal date avoids additional fees.

3. Taxes

Applicable taxes, such as VAT, will be applied to all invoices where required by law. Taxes are calculated based on the total amount of the service plus any applicable fees.

Example:

Service Price VAT (10%)
VPS G 1 $6.64 $0.56
Activation Fee $2.50 $0.25

4. Billing Period

Each invoice covers a one-month billing period. The period starts on the service activation date or the renewal date for ongoing services.

  • Invoices are generated automatically before the start of the next billing period.
  • Customers are responsible for ensuring timely payment to avoid service interruption.
  • Any unpaid invoices after the due date may incur suspension and reactivation fees.

5. Service Cancellation & Refunds

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted before the renewal date to avoid charges for the next billing period.
  • No refunds are issued for partial months unless specified.
  • Cancelled services billed due to invoice generation prior to cancellation may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • Once a service is cancelled, access to the VPS and its data will be terminated at the end of the current billing period.

6. Late Payment

  • Invoices must be paid by the due date to avoid service suspension.
  • Suspended services may require reactivation fees to resume.
  • Repeated late payments may result in termination of account or services.

7. Terms & Conditions

By using our VPS services, you agree to the terms outlined in this billing policy. These policies may be updated periodically, and customers are responsible for reviewing the latest version.

  • All payments are non-transferable and non-refundable unless specified.
  • Any disputes regarding billing must be submitted within 7 days of invoice issuance.
  • The company reserves the right to modify fees, taxes, and billing procedures with prior notice.
Note: Always ensure your payment method is active and up-to-date to avoid unexpected suspension or fees. Your understanding of this billing policy ensures smooth service continuity.
