Frequently asked questions

Is Astro free to use? +

Yes! Astro is included free with all Nutro Cloud hosting plans. Just log in to your dashboard at pro.nutro.cloud and start chatting.

Does Astro support Arabic? +

Absolutely. Astro is fully fluent in both English and Arabic. You can ask questions, give commands, and receive answers in either language.

Can Astro make changes to my server? +

Yes. Astro can enable caching, configure DNS, manage SSL certificates, create email accounts, and more — all through simple chat commands. It always asks for confirmation before making critical changes.

What if Astro can't solve my problem? +

If Astro encounters something beyond its capabilities, it seamlessly escalates to our human support team with full context — so you never have to repeat yourself.

Is my data safe with Astro? +

100%. Astro operates within your hosting environment only. Your conversations are encrypted, never shared with third parties, and you can delete your chat history anytime.
