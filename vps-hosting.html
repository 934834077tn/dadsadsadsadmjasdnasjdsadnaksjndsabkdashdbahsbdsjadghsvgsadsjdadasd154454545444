VPS Hosting — Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cloud VPS infrastructure.

What is VPS hosting and who needs it?+
VPS (Virtual Private Server) hosting gives you dedicated resources — CPU, RAM, and NVMe storage — on a virtual server with full root access, completely isolated from other users. It's ideal for growing websites, web applications, developers, agencies managing multiple client sites, e-commerce stores, game servers, or anyone who needs more power and control than shared hosting offers.
How quickly is my VPS ready after ordering?+
Your VPS deploys in approximately 60 seconds. Choose your operating system (Ubuntu, Debian, CentOS, AlmaLinux), select a data center from 8 global locations, pick your CPU/RAM/storage specs, and you'll have full SSH root access immediately.
Can I upgrade CPU, RAM, or storage without downtime?+
Absolutely. Upgrade CPU, RAM, or storage instantly from your dashboard with zero downtime. Our live migration technology ensures your applications stay online throughout the upgrade process — no reboots required.
Is enterprise DDoS protection included?+
Yes. Enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation protecting against L3/L4/L7 attacks is included free on every VPS plan. The protection is always-on, zero-configuration, and handles both volumetric and application-layer attacks automatically.
What hardware powers Nutro Cloud VPS?+
All servers run on latest-generation AMD EPYC 9004 processors with DDR5 ECC RAM and NVMe Gen4 SSD storage delivering up to 7,000 MB/s sequential read. Network connectivity is 1 Gbps dedicated per VPS with redundant Tier 1 transit uplinks.
What data center locations are available?+
We offer 8 data center locations across 4 continents: New York (USA), London (UK), Paris (France), Frankfurt (Germany), Amsterdam (Netherlands), Singapore, Mumbai (India), and São Paulo (Brazil). Choose the closest location to your audience for sub-20ms latency.
Do you provide managed VPS support?+
Yes! We offer both unmanaged (you handle the server) and managed options (we handle OS updates, security patches, monitoring, and optimization). All plans include 24/7/365 expert support via live chat and ticket system.
